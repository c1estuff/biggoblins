Welcome to Big Goblins

A Community-Driven OSRS Clan Where Everyone Belongs

Founded in 2021, Big Goblins has grown into one of OSRS's most welcoming and active communities. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or just starting your journey in Gielinor, our doors are always open.

Thriving Community

Join over 200 active members who share your passion for OSRS. From casual conversations to epic boss hunts, there's always something happening in our clan.

PVM & Bossing

Looking to learn Raids? Want to master Vorkath? Our experienced PVMers are here to help you conquer any boss. Regular teaching events and group bossing make the grind more enjoyable.

Skilling Together

Regular Events

Big Goblin's Rules – Clan Rules

Respect the Goblin Code

Treat fellow clan members with respect. Trash talk is fine, but keep it friendly—no excessive toxicity, racism, or harassment. We're here to game, not babysit drama.

No Scamming or Begging

Don't scam clan members or randoms—it makes us all look bad. If you're broke, go kill some goblins or do some skilling.

Help Your Goblin Bros

If a clanmate needs help with a boss, a quest, or just some advice, lend a hand if you can. We grow stronger together.

No Leeching in Clan Events

If we're doing a raid, a mass, or a PK trip, pull your weight. Don't sit there like a goblin in Lumbridge waiting for scraps.

PKing is Allowed, But No Backstabbing

We support PKing, but don't skull-trick or betray clan members. If you set up a fight with another clanmate, make sure both parties agree beforehand.

Loot Splitting & Item Lending

Loot splits and item lending are at player discretion—the clan is NOT responsible for stolen items or someone backing out of a split. If you scam a clanmate, you will be reported to RuneWatch and anywhere else that flags scammers. If proof is provided, you will be removed from the clan. Play fair, or get blacklisted.

No Botting, RWT, or Exploits

Don't bring Jagex's hammer down on the clan. Play fair, or at least be smart about it.

Keep the Chat Goblin-Friendly

We allow dark humor and jokes, but don't cross into excessive hate speech or constant toxicity. Know where the line is.

Clan Leadership Has Final Say

If a leader or ranked member makes a call, respect it. If you have issues, bring them up properly, not in the middle of a clan event.

Most Importantly – Have Fun

We're here to have a good time, slay bosses, make GP, and dominate the game. If you ain't enjoying yourself, you're doing it wrong.

No Explicit Content

Keep all shared content and images appropriate. No explicit, NSFW, or inappropriate content in any clan channels or communications. This includes profile pictures, shared media, and chat content.
